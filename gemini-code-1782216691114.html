Made by Human Hands, Molded with Love

MUGELLE is born from a desire to turn mundane mornings into whimsical cottagecore dreams. We don't believe in industrial perfection.

Every pinch, every hand-carved cherry pattern, and every 3D clay ribbon handle bears the genuine imprint of human hands. We use rich earth clay to create cute companions that bring joy, art, and slow living into your home space.
